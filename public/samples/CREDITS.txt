Drum samples in this directory are sourced from the Tone.js audio
collection (https://github.com/Tonejs/audio/tree/master/drum-samples),
which in turn derives them from Chris Wilson's web-audio-samples
(https://github.com/cwilso/web-audio-samples).

Kits used:
  lofi/        — CR78    (Roland CR-78 drum machine sounds)
  tribal/      — Bongos  (hand percussion)
  electronic/  — Stark   (modern punchy kit)

Each kit's three files map to the engine's piece slots:
  kick.mp3   → kick voice
  snare.mp3  → snare/clap voice
  hat.mp3    → hi-hat voice

The upstream sources do not carry an explicit open-source license.
They have been broadly used in Web Audio tutorials and examples since
~2012. If you intend to ship this app commercially, replace these with
samples you have explicit rights to distribute.
